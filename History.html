Pasta wasnt always tomatoes—tomatoes arrived very late.

Tomatoes are native to the Americas and only reached Italy in the 16th century. For centuries before that, Italians ate pasta with cheese, pepper, or other sauces—no marinara in sight.

Rome had a fire department… run by a private millionaire?

In ancient Rome, Marcus Licinius Crassus—one of the richest men in history— created his own fire brigade. When a building caught fire, his team would show up and only put it out if the owner agreed to sell the property to Crassus at a discounted price.
