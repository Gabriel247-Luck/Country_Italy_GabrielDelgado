History of Italy

From the Roman Empire to the Renaissance

Home History Culture Food

Italy has one of the richest histories in the world. It was once the heart of the Roman Empire, which influenced modern government, law, and engineering. Ancient ruins like the Colosseum still stand today.

During the Renaissance, Italy became the center of art and innovation. Famous figures such as Leonardo da Vinci, Michelangelo, and Galileo helped shape modern science and art.
