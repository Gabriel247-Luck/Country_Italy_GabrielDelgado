Italy is a fascinating country in Europe, it is shaped in form of a boot, with beaches like the Mediterranean. Its part of the Roman Empire, with it being the beginning of the Renaissance, with very important figures like Leonardo da Vinci and Michelangelo. Italy also has some of the best food like pasta and pizza, fantastic art and buildings, and some of the worlds most known places like the Colosseum and the Leaning Tower of Pisa. Its also part of beautiful places, from mountains in the north to beaches in the south. Italy is known for its unique and deep history with modern historical culture, fashion, and technology, that makes it a must-see for others wanting to learn about Italy for the very first time.
