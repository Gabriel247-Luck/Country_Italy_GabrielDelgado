5 Foods that you should tryout when visiting Italy.

1. TRY THEIR FAMOUS PIZZA MARGHERITA.

Hailing from Naples, this classic pizza features simple, fresh toppings of tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil, representing the Italian flag's colors.

2. SPAGHETTI ALLA CARBONARA.

A Roman pasta dish made with eggs, hard cheese (Like Parmesan), cured pork, and black pepper.

3. LASAGNE AL FORNO.

Lasagne al Forno: Hearty baked pasta with layers of pasta sheets, rich ragù (meat sauce), creamy béchamel sauce, and cheese.

4. RISOTTO ALLA MILANESE.

A Northern Italian specialty from Milan, known for its vibrant golden color from saffron and its creamy texture.

5. TIRAMISU.

A popular coffee-flavored dessert with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers, mascarpone cheese, and cocoa powder.
